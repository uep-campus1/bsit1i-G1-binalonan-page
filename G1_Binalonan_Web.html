About Binalonan

Overview

Binalonan is a first class municipality in Pangasinan known for its peaceful community, steady progress, and active citizen participation.

Mission

We, through good governance, are committed to nurture and inspire the lives of our constituents and partners in development alike by creating an exceptional environment that promotes opportunities for diverse business and employment, quality education, happy and healthy families, safe and peaceful neighborhoods, sustainable and green communities, thriving culture and the arts and more fun and leisure recreations thru development of tourism sites and activities.

Vision

In 2032, Binalonan is the premier economic center of 5th District of Pangasinan bolstered by educational, commercial, industrial, agricultural and tourism drivers while providing a vibrant and safe environment for its culturally rich and responsible citizens.

Values

• Maka-Diyos (Godliness, uprightness)
• Marunong (Educated, wise, skilled, hardworking, industrious)
• Malinis (Clean, honest, orderly)

History

Binalonan was established during the Spanish period and has grown from a small agricultural town into a developing municipality. It remains rooted in its cultural heritage while embracing modernization and development.
